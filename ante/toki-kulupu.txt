1. i'm sorry

1. i understand

mi supa -
1. i'm laying down

1. i'm leaving

1. if people can understand it, it's right

1. goodbye, farewell

o moku e kala pona -
1. goodbye, farewell

1. do what you want, do what you think is good

1. work is bad

1. what's poppin'?

1. hyped, energetic, exciting

weka tawa tenpo kama -
